Dengue Prevention Tips

Follow these measures to protect yourself and your community from dengue fever.

Eliminate Stagnant Water

Remove or regularly clean containers that hold water to prevent mosquito breeding, such as flower pots, buckets, and tires.

Use Mosquito Repellents

Apply approved mosquito repellents on exposed skin, especially during early morning and evening hours when mosquitoes are active.

Use Mosquito Nets

Sleep under mosquito nets and cover windows and doors with screens to prevent mosquito entry indoors.

Keep Surroundings Clean

Maintain cleanliness in and around your home, clearing debris and garbage where mosquitoes can hide and breed.

Seek Early Medical Attention

If you experience dengue symptoms like high fever, headache, joint pain, or rashes, consult a doctor immediately.

Community Awareness

Participate in local dengue control programs and educate neighbors about preventive measures to reduce dengue spread.
