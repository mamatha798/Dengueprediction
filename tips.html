Dengue Prevention Tips

Follow these measures to protect yourself and your community from dengue fever.

Eliminate Stagnant Water

Remove or clean containers that hold water to prevent mosquito breeding.

Use Mosquito Repellents

Apply repellents on exposed skin, especially during mosquito-active hours.

Use Mosquito Nets

Sleep under mosquito nets and install screens on windows and doors.

Keep Surroundings Clean

Clear garbage and debris where mosquitoes can hide.

Seek Medical Attention

If symptoms appear, consult a doctor immediately.

Community Awareness

Educate others and support local dengue control initiatives.
