Dengue Prevention Tips

Follow these measures to protect yourself and your community from dengue fever.

Use Mosquito Repellents

Apply mosquito repellent on exposed skin to reduce mosquito bites.

Use Mosquito Nets

Always sleep under mosquito nets and install window screens.

Seek Medical Attention

If symptoms appear, consult a doctor immediately.

Community Awareness

Participate in dengue awareness programs and educate others.
