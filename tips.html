Dengue Prevention Tips

Follow these measures to protect yourself and your community from dengue fever.

Use Mosquito Repellents

Apply mosquito repellents on exposed skin to reduce mosquito bites and protect yourself.

Use Mosquito Nets

Sleep under mosquito nets and install insect screens on doors and windows.

Seek Medical Attention

If you notice symptoms like fever or rash, visit a doctor immediately to avoid complications.

Community Awareness

Spread awareness and participate in local campaigns to control mosquito breeding.
